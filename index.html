How to Use

1. Enter Ai Price (BTC): current Ai price in Amadon.

2. Each item has 3 inputs:

• Amadon Price: sell price to Amadon. Check the Amadon menu in-game.

• P2P Rate: Item-to-Ai ratio (e.g. 1 TSC = 105 Ai). Ask other players / check market chat.

• Qty: how many items you have / want to sell.

3. Result: Net BTC = (Amadon or Rate×Ai) − Crafting Cost. Green = Amadon wins, blue = Market wins.

4. BEP Ai = the Ai price at which Market beats Amadon. If current Ai < BEP → sell to Amadon.
